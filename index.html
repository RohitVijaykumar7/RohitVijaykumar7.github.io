About Us

Savitar: The God of Speed

Named after the Hindu God of Speed, Savitar Racing is an F1 in Schools team from the Colchester Royal Grammar School. Comprised of six students between Year 10 and Year 12, we are a team of ambitious, hard-working students with only one aim: To win. We are looking for sponsors, so if you are considering sponsoring us, please contact us using the links below.

Meet Our Team

REI OTA

Rei Ota, as Team Leader, is responsible for managing the team, project management of all key deliverables and ensuring that allrace cars are ready for the competition. He works closely with all members of the team, offering assistance where necessary.

ARAV SWATI-ABHAY

Arav is our Design Engineer. He is responsible for the styling and aerodynamic performance of the car design. He works with Adwait, the manufacturing engineer, to ensure their ideas can be realised.

GEORGE ROBERTSON

George is our Graphic Designer, and so responsible for producing the colour schemes applied to the vehicle, including any special sponsorship decals, together with the final graphic renderings. He works with the design engineer to ensure any schemes will fit the shape of the vehicle.

ROHIT VIJAYKUMAR

Rohit, as our Sponsorship and Marketing Manager, is responsible for generating sponsorship proposals, contacting firms and marketing the team. He is also tasked with creating and managing the teamâ€™s social media accounts, as well as designing and building this website and creating our team logo.

Oliver Lee

As Finance Manager, Oliver organises budgets and resources needed for designing and making the cars and team project work. He also keeps track of all expenses and income generated, allocating certain funds to certain areas of the project, for example the cars, Pit Display and the portfolio.

Adwait Vashista

Adwait is our Manufacturing Engineer, and is responsible for advising team members on the manufacture of the car and the constraints of the machining process. He works with the design engineer Arav to report and help solve any problems with the construction of the car.
